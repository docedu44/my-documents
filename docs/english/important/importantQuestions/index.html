سوالات مهم

سوالات زیر، سوالاتی هستند که در صحبت های روزمره زیاد استفاده می شود:


What is your name?  ==========  my name is mohammad.

How old are you?  ==========  I'm 26.

What do you do?  ==========  I'm an engineer.

Where do you live?  ==========  I live in Karaj.

What is your favorite color?  ==========  my favorite color is red.  ||  my favorite colors are red, blue and green.

What is your favorite sport?  ==========  My favorite sport is score.  ||  My favorite sports are swimming and mountain climbing.

How many siblings do you have?  ==========  I have 2 sisters and 3 brothers.

What does your brother do?  ==========  He is a driver.

what does your dad do?  ==========  He is retired.
